FAQ

Do I need technical skills to manage my online store?

Not at all! We build your store with an intuitive admin panel that's as easy as using social media. You can add products, update prices, and manage orders with simple clicks. We provide free training videos and offer 24/7 support to help you with any questions.

What payment methods can my customers use?

We integrate all major payment options: credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and even buy-now-pay-later services. For Thailand specifically, we support PromptPay, TrueMoney, and bank transfers. Multiple currencies are supported for international sales.

How fast can I start selling after signing up?

Your store can be live within 48-72 hours! We handle all the technical setup, payment integration, and initial product uploads (up to 50 products). Once launched, you can start accepting orders immediately. Most clients make their first sale within the first week.

What about shipping and inventory management?

Our system automatically tracks inventory levels and alerts you when stock is low. We integrate with major shipping providers (DHL, FedEx, local couriers) for real-time shipping rates and automated tracking emails. You can also set up dropshipping if you don't hold inventory.

Will my store work well on mobile phones?

Absolutely! Over 70% of online shopping happens on mobile devices. Your store will be fully optimized for smartphones with fast loading, easy navigation, and one-click checkout. We also include features like saved carts and mobile payment options to maximize mobile conversions.
