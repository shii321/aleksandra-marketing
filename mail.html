How much does email marketing cost for small businesses?

How often should I send emails to my customers?

For restaurants and salons, we recommend 2-4 emails per month. This includes weekly specials, appointment reminders, birthday offers, and seasonal promotions. We optimize frequency based on your audience engagement to avoid email fatigue.
