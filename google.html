Google Ads Platform

Main platform for creating and managing all types of advertising campaigns in Google's ecosystem. Set up search ads, banners, video ads and remarketing here.

Google Analytics 4

Tracks every user action on your website and shows which ads actually lead to sales. Without this, you're spending money blindly.

Google Tag Manager

Allows adding tracking codes to your site without a programmer. Set it up once — now you can track any user actions with a few clicks.

Smart Bidding (AI)

Google's AI automatically raises or lowers bids for clicks in real time. The machine learns from billions of signals and saves your budget better than humans.

Video advertising to 2+ billion YouTube users with precise targeting by interests and behavior. Perfect for brand awareness and engagement.

Product ads with photos, prices and ratings directly in search results. Buyers see your product before clicking — 26% higher conversion rate.

FAQ

How quickly will I see results from Google Ads?

Your ads start running immediately after approval (usually within 24 hours). You'll see traffic and clicks right away, with meaningful conversions typically beginning within the first week of campaign optimization.

What's the minimum budget needed for Google Ads?

We recommend starting with $20-30 per day ($600-900/month) for effective testing and optimization. This budget allows us to gather sufficient data and compete effectively in most industries.

Do you guarantee specific ROI from Google Ads campaigns?

While we can't guarantee specific returns due to market variables, our typical client achieves 300-500% ROI within 90 days. We offer transparent reporting and a 60-day satisfaction guarantee on our management services.

Which Google Ads campaign types work best for my business?

This depends on your goals. Search campaigns are great for immediate sales, Shopping ads work best for e-commerce, and Display/Video campaigns excel at brand awareness. We analyze your business and recommend the optimal mix.

Can I track exactly where my Google Ads leads come from?

Absolutely! We set up comprehensive conversion tracking that shows you exactly which keywords, ads, and campaigns generate your best leads. You'll get detailed weekly reports with cost-per-lead and ROI data for every campaign.
